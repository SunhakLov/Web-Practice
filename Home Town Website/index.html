Top three activities to do at Battambang

Visit a Museum

The Battambang Provincial Museum (also known as the Battambang Museum) offers a captivating journey through Cambodia’s rich sculptural heritage

Riding on a Bamboo Train

Riding the Bamboo Train—locally called the norry—in Battambang is one of those uniquely thrilling experiences that perfectly captures the spirit of rural Cambodia

Visit Banan Temple

Perched atop a serene hill just outside Battambang, Banan Temple (Phnom Banan) offers a perfect blend of history, spirituality, and breathtaking scenery.
